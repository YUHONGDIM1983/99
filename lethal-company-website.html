ABOUT THE GAME

Lethal Company is a thrilling co-op horror survival game where you and your friends work as contractors for the Company, salvaging abandoned facilities on various moons for valuable scrap.

You'll need to meet your quota by scavenging enough valuable items, but be careful - you're not alone. Nightmarish creatures lurk in the darkness, ready to make your mission your last. Balance risk and reward as you decide how long to stay and how much loot to carry.

Work together with your team, use your tools wisely, and try to survive the horrors that await you in the abandoned facilities. Will you make it back alive with enough profit to satisfy the Company?
